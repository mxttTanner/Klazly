# Why your center needs Cổng Phụ Huynh

**A one-page case for English language centers in Vietnam.**

---

## The problem you live with every week

Most English centers in Vietnam still run parent communication on three tools that don't talk to each other:

- 📓 **Paper notebooks** for attendance, homework, and notes
- 💬 **Zalo group chats** that flood with photos, replies and one-off questions — important info gets buried within hours
- 📊 **Excel sheets** scattered across teacher laptops, no shared view

What that costs you, every single week:

| Pain | What it actually means |
|---|---|
| Teachers spend 5–10 hours/week writing reports + replying to Zalo messages individually | Teacher burnout, less prep time |
| Parents have no clear picture of how their child is doing | When they doubt — they leave for another center |
| Owner can't tell which teachers are recording lessons | No data, no accountability |
| Reports are inconsistent across teachers | Weakens your brand vs Apax / ILA / VUS |
| Records lost when teachers quit or notebooks are misplaced | No institutional memory |

If even one of these costs you a single student per quarter, you've lost more than the platform's annual price.

---

## What Cổng Phụ Huynh does

A web platform built specifically for Vietnamese English centers. Three connected views, one source of truth.

### 👨‍💼 For you (center owner)
- See every class on one dashboard, grouped by program (KET / IELTS / Communication / your own categories)
- See which teachers are recording lessons consistently — and which aren't
- Manage classes, students, parents, teachers from one place
- Your logo + your branding on the parent portal and printed reports
- Step in for absent teachers — open any class, record the lesson, no chasing required

### 🎓 For your teachers
- Open phone after class, record a lesson in 60 seconds
- "Start from last lesson" copies your previous lesson — change date + per-student notes
- Save reusable lesson templates (vocab, grammar, speaking, homework — all on tap)
- Bulk apply "everyone did homework" then mark the few who didn't — no clicking 12 students
- Attach PDF worksheets parents can download
- Mark each student's overall level so you spot who needs attention

### 👨‍👩‍👧 For parents
- Open browser on phone — see the unit/lesson/topic of every class their child took
- See behavior rating, your teacher's note, homework status, lesson worksheet
- One tap → printable PDF report with **your center's letterhead** + custom intro and signature lines
- They share the PDF on Zalo with grandparents → your branding spreads

### 🌐 Bilingual
- Vietnamese is the default, English toggle one click — works for foreign teachers
- Per-center data isolation (Row Level Security) — bank-level separation between centers
